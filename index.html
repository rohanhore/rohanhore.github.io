Welcome to my homepage!

I am a fifth year Phd student in the Department of Statistics at the University of Chicago, supervised by Professor Rina Foygel Barber. Prior to this, I graduated from the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata with a bachleor's and master's degree in Statistics. My research interests are in distribution-free inference, multiple testing and variable selection. My current research primarily focuses on developing methods for navigating through the hardness of model-free inference.
